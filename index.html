My Fitness Routine Cards

This page shows a simple weekly fitness routine.

Morning Stretch

Start the day with simple stretches for your arms, legs, back, and neck.

Time: 10 minutes

Cardio Workout

Do jumping jacks, jogging, or skipping to improve stamina and energy.

Time: 20 minutes

Strength Training

Practice squats, push-ups, and planks to build body strength.

Time: 15 minutes

Yoga Time

Try simple yoga poses to improve balance, flexibility, and focus.

Time: 15 minutes

Evening Walk

Go for a relaxing walk to stay active and refresh your mind.

Time: 25 minutes

Rest and Hydrate

Drink enough water and take proper rest after completing your workout.

Time: All day

Fitness Tip: A good routine includes exercise, rest, healthy food, and consistency.
